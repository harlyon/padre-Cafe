Our uniqueness is Our Specialty....

The restaurant was created with a specific reason, which is to stand out from the crowd and be unique in its taste. The concept is delightful and it mixes both live entertainment with great tasting foods and drinks. There is a magnificent cool bar where we offer special drinks and desserts. In addition to the bar, we offer breakfast items, snacks, tea, coffee, desserts and drinks. Moreover, Kellogs also provides catering services.

Firstly, our food and hot beverages have been developed by our creative chefs to attract customers who like the eastern taste, but with a modern and contemporary twist. On the food side, all the dishes are made with eastern flavours in mind but with a touch of the west. We are continually developing new products, which will continue this ethos of east and west fusion..
